FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get my CBT login password?▼
Your CBT password is issued by your class teacher. If you haven't received it yet, use the contact form above or ask your teacher directly. The demo password for testing is student123.
Can I access the website from my phone?▼
Yes! EduReach is fully mobile-friendly. You can use any smartphone, tablet, or computer with a web browser. No app download is required.
What happens when I click "Start Test"?▼
After logging in, clicking "Start Test" will redirect you to a Google Form containing the CBT questions. Ensure you have a stable internet connection before starting, as the test must be completed in one sitting.
Are the study materials free?▼
Yes, all study materials on EduReach are completely free for students. Simply visit the "Explore" page and browse resources by subject.
I forgot my password. What should I do?▼
Use the contact form on this page, select "Forgot Password" as the topic, and your teacher will reset it for you within 24 hours.
